Open

Powerful

Customizable

Ocean is a decentralized platform.
Ocean is just a platform for server owners to make their own instances of Ocean, and they can all talk to each other! There's large instances for the masses, smaller ones for groups, fandoms, and subcultures, and even instances just for individuals and their friends. Want to have even more control? You can make your own instance on your server with relative ease, cutting ties from any large company who wants to spy on you and sell your data. Ocean is about community and sharing, not corporations and big tech. Have friends not on Ocean? No problem! Because Ocean is on the Fediverse (ActivityPub), you can interact with people on other platforms like Mastodon, PixelFed, PeerTube, and more!
